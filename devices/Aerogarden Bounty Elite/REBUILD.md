# Rebuild Notes
Rebuilding with the base components isn't terribly complicated.  All you need to drive are the LEDs and the water pump.  
# Components
* DC Step Down Module {https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DSYXLKZT?ref=ppx_yo2ov_dt_b_fed_asin_title}
* DC Boost Converter Module {https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FDK8N8TZ?ref=ppx_yo2ov_dt_b_fed_asin_title}
* Relay {https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00VRUAHLE?ref_=ppx_hzsearch_conn_dt_b_fed_asin_title_4}

# Tinkering Notes
- Connecting the LEDs directly to power causes them to flicker.  I suspect another component is needed (capacitor)?
- The Control Board won't fully power on unless it's connected to the Touch Panel.  I tried to figure out what connectivity I needed to replicate to get the Control Board to work indpendently.  Ultimately I let the magic smoke out.  Now I'm rebuilding it with basic components.  More to come!
