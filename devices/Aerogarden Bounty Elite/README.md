# Aerogarden Bounty Elite

## Why?

The touch panel got wet and stopped responding, making it unusable.  Even worse, it would beep loudly at regular intervals preventing us from leaving it plugged in.

## Inside at a Glance
* Control Board
* Power Board
* Touch Panel

## Worth Voiding?
Yes.  In my case, the device was no longer usable.  It's easy enough to disassemble and there's plenty of space inside for a raspberry pi. 

## Notes
- Touch screen is proprietary and there's basically zero chance of reusing it.  Could not find any replacements
